Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Allah & I is a personal journaling app. Your privacy is fundamental to everything we do.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app has no servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.

Journal Entries

Your journal entries are stored locally on your device in a private database. They never leave your device unless you choose to enable sync.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your entries are stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. Only you can access this data — not even the app developer can see it. Apple handles encryption in transit and at rest.

Google Drive Sync

If you enable Google Drive sync, your entries are encrypted on your device using AES-256 encryption before being uploaded to your personal Google Drive. The encryption key is derived from your Google account and never leaves your device. Even if someone accessed your Google Drive files, they would not be able to read your entries.

Biometric Data

If you enable biometric lock, the app uses your device's Face ID or Touch ID to authenticate you. Biometric data is handled entirely by your device's operating system — the app never sees, stores, or transmits biometric information.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

Anonymous Crash & Performance Data

The app collects anonymous crash reports and performance metrics (such as app launch time) to help us identify and fix issues. This data contains no personal information, no journal content, and cannot be used to identify you. It is collected through Expo Insights and is used solely to improve app quality.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Expo Insights for anonymous crash and performance monitoring. No personal data or journal content is included. The only other external services are Apple iCloud and Google Drive, used only when you explicitly enable sync. The app contains no advertising.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
