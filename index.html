Allah & I

One question. Every day.

How was my relationship with Allah today?

That's it. No prompts. No ratings. No gratitude lists. No AI. Just a black screen, a serif font, and space to write.

Every entry begins with Oh Allah...

The way you spoke to your mother. The patience you had — or didn't. The forgiveness you gave. The grudge you held. Every relationship you have with another person is a reflection of the one you have with Him.

Talk about anything. Your prayers. Your doubts. Your gratitude. Your silence. The du'a you made. The moment you felt close. The day you felt nothing at all.

This is your honest conversation with Allah — about all of it.

Private first

Like your relationship with Allah, this is completely private. Your entries are stored locally on your device — nowhere else. If you choose to sync across devices, your data is encrypted and visible to no one. Not even us.

What it is

  • One question, one entry, every day
  • A calendar to look back on your journey
  • Sync across devices with iCloud or Google Drive
  • No account required

What it is not

  • Not a mood tracker
  • Not a gratitude journal
  • Not an AI companion
  • Not a social app
  • Not complicated
